Igor Sakharov

Russian Advertising Photographer Igor Sakharov is one of the best commercial photographers in Russia. He is the winner of many international advertising/commercial photography competitions. Besides, he is a member of the "Guild of Advertising Photographers". He is known as a photographer, capable of completing a shot of any complexity. Igor Sakharov is the first Russian photographer who received the title of “Qualified European Photographer of advertising”.
He studied in Germany. He was in the advertising business – over 15 years. Igor described his experience like this-"If there is no goal, it will seem wasting time."

These three product shots captured my attention as an original and unique shot, and I was getting inspired by the lighting technique that he used in all his shots. Working at the negative background and turning to bright background seems to be very extraordinary and eye-catching.
